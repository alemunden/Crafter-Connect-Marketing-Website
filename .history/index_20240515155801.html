About our App

Crafters Connect is a collaborative platform for any and all Do-It-Yourself projects from home improvement to everyday crafting. Create a central place to share anything and everything about your project, and invite other collaborators to help work with you, share their project, and update important milestones. Once you've completed something, why not share it with the world? Crafters Connect features a community of creators and crafters just like you who are eager to put their creations out there using our dedicated community forum and posts. Whether you are a real hard worker, a casual crafter, or even just someone who likes to help out others, Crafters Connect has a place for you.
